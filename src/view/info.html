Product Scraper

Do a GET request to the route http://localhost:3009/api/scrap?url=[product_URL] to retrieve the expected information.

Example:

http://localhost:3009/api/scrap?url=https://www.saraiva.com.br/fifa-22-br-ps4-20074001/p


Ps: For now is only available to scrape Zattine, Saraiva and CasasBahia.


URLs examples:

https://www.saraiva.com.br/fifa-22-br-ps4-20074001/p

https://www.zattini.com.br/vesitod-top-moda-chemise-midi-preto-ESJ-0296-006
